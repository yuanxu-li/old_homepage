Interests

I have been playing guitar since high school, still an amateur though. The very motivation that triggers me to learn to play guitar is Surface by Cheer Chen. I do not play guitar as often as I used to, and as a result, I do not expect too much progress in future. But the thing that matters is, it always accompanies me as a friend, whenever I need. Singing and writing songs are my favorite way of expressing myself.

I read a lot, mostly novels. My favorite writer is Haruki Murakami, a Japanese novelist. Though he is not a Nobel laureate yet, many people have been predicting that he would be the next Nobel prize winner in literature, for several years.
